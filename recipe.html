HEAVENLY WAFFLES

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter (or oil)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix flour, sugar, eggs, milk, and melted butter into a smooth batter.
  2. Preheat the waffle iron until hot.
  3. Lightly grease the waffle plates.
  4. Pour some batter and close the lid.
  5. Cook until golden, then serve with toppings.
